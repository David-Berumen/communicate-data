What is the structure of your dataset?¶

The dataframe contains 24 columns and 129880. 5 columns are string and the rest are integers.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

The main feature is the overall satisfaction which takes the value of "satisfied" and "neutral or dissatisfied".

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

I am interested in seing the impact travel distance, class and delays has on the satisfaction, another interesting feature to investigate is the age. I don't expect a very strong relation between a specific satisfaction factors and the overall satisfaction since I think they only affect in extreme cases.
